# Algorithmic_Fairness_2026

## IT University of Copenhagen (Spring 2026)

AFAE course is an overview on the topics of bias and fairness in data, models, and algorithms, and on the associated ethical and accountability issues. **This repository** contains exercises for [Spring semester 2026](https://learnit.itu.dk/course/view.php?id=3025445).

### Lectures and exercises

1. Introduction
2. Fairness metrics
3. Explainability: Motivation and White-box models
4. Explainability: Black-box models
5. Bias sources
6. Debiasing models
7. The Ethics of Fairness
8. Ethics on a Societal Scale
9. Philosophy of big data
10. Debiasing data
11. Robustness of Models
12. Auditing Algorithms
13. Fairness & Generative AI
